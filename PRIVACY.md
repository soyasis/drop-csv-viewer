# Privacy Policy

**Drop CSV Viewer**
_Last updated: June 2026_

Drop CSV Viewer displays local CSV and TSV files as a table in your browser.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** Drop CSV Viewer does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any data.

- Files you open are read and processed **entirely on your device**, via the browser's File API.
- They are **never uploaded** and never leave your computer.
- The extension makes **no network requests** and includes **no analytics or tracking**.
- It gathers no personal information, browsing history, or usage data.

A CSV or TSV file you open is parsed and shown only in the extension's own tab, and is discarded when you close it.

## Permissions

Drop CSV Viewer requests access to all sites for one reason: so you can drag a CSV/TSV file onto any page to view it. Its content script reacts solely to dropped `.csv`/`.tsv` files — it does not read page content, cookies, or form data.
